# 3. Co-Founder Exit Clause

> **CO-FOUNDER / CO-DEVELOPER EXIT CLAUSE** — *PTP-102 Equine Technology Platform*  
> *(An Addendum to the Founder Agreement)*  
> *Defines clear exit terms for regulatory, clinical, and commercial partners.*

---

## PREAMBLE

This Exit Clause is entered into by and between the co-founders / co-developers of **BYROCK CLINICAL LTD** (the “Company”) dated __________, and outlines the terms for when a co-founder exits the Company.

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

- **1.1 “Exiting Founder / Developer”** — Any co-founder/co-developer who ceases to be actively involved for any reason.
- **1.2 “Active Involvement”** — Working full-time or contributing substantively to regulatory submissions, clinical trial operations, or commercial licensing.

---

## 2. EXIT EVENTS

This clause is triggered if a co-founder:
- **(a)** Voluntarily resigns or exits the Company.
- **(b)** Is removed by majority decision of the Board due to breach of duties.
- **(c)** Fails to perform duties for more than 60 consecutive days without valid reason.
- **(d)** Passes away or becomes permanently incapacitated.

---

## 3. EQUITY & OWNERSHIP

- **3.1** Upon exit, the Exiting Founder forfeits all unvested equity / licence interest units.
- **3.2** Vested equity is subject to the vesting schedule per the original Founder Agreement.
- **3.3** Remaining founders have the **right of first refusal** to purchase the Exiting Founder’s vested shares at fair market value.
- **3.4** Purchase price shall be determined by mutual agreement or an independent valuer.

---

## 4. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS

- **4.1 Non-Compete:** The Exiting Founder cannot compete with the Company in equine clinical technology, veterinary diagnostics, or related licensing for **12 months** from the exit date, subject to applicable law.
- **4.2 Non-Solicitation:** Cannot solicit employees, customers, or investors for **12 months**.
- **4.3 Confidentiality:** Must maintain confidentiality of Company information (clinical data, regulatory submissions, IP, partner lists) in perpetuity.
- **4.4 Data & Sample Return:** All regulatory dossiers, clinical trial datasets, and proprietary sample handling protocols must be returned or destroyed within 14 days.

---

## 5. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

- **5.1** This clause is governed by the laws of **Republic of Ireland** [or applicable jurisdiction].
- **5.2** Any dispute shall be resolved by arbitration in accordance with applicable rules.
