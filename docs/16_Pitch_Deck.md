## 1. THE PROBLEM

Equine lameness and inflammatory conditions are:
- **Misdiagnosed:** Subjective visual scoring leads to variable outcomes.
- **Fragmented:** Disconnected diagnostics, lab workflows, and clinician tools.
- **Expensive:** Late diagnosis increases treatment cost and downtime for performance and breeding horses.

---

## 2. OUR SOLUTION

PTP-102 is an integrated equine clinical platform that:
- Standardises lameness and inflammatory biomarker assessment.
- Links veterinary clinics, laboratories, and owners via a secure digital workflow.
- Generates regulatory-grade documentation and traceability.
