## 2. REGULATORY PATHWAY CONTEXT

### 2.1 United States (Primary Market)
- **Regulatory Consultant:** Dr. Mark Hughes, Hughes Veterinary Consulting (HVC). HVC is registered in the FDA CVM database and serves as Byrock’s US Agent.
- **Pathway:** Conditional New Animal Drug Application (CNADA) under the MUMS pathway.
  - Conditional approval allows marketing with a **Reasonable Expectation of Effectiveness (RXE)** study (reduced patient numbers and formulation flexibility) vs. full Substantial Evidence.
  - Sponsor has **5 years** post-conditional approval to continue progress toward full approval.
- **Fee Status:** Minor-Use Fee Waiver requested. If granted, waives the annual sponsor fee and other FDA fees.
- **Timeline (Mark Hughes baseline):** ~3.5 years from CVM engagement to conditional approval — i.e., **3Q28 target** for conditional FDA approval.
- **CVM Engagement:** Draft PSC request and Claim of Categorical Exclusion complete; meeting request prepared for 1Q25; CVM response expected within ~6 months per technical section.

### 2.2 UAE (Parallel Early-Access Track)
- **Strategy:** Early commercial entry via UAE (Dubai Equine Hospital), generating field data to support US program.
- **Registration:** Two-stage registration — (1) manufacturing site, (2) product — aligned with MOCCAE requirements.
- **Supply:** 50 g pilot supply from Chinese GMP site prepared for shipment to Dubai Equine Hospital (UAE veterinary pilot study, ~10 horses).

### 2.3 European Union
- EU registration follows VICH guidelines (EU is a VICH founding member).
- A prospective European partner has indicated interest in exclusive continental Europe rights; deal structuring requires alignment on EU-specific CMC data packages.

## 7. QUALITY & COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

1. **FDA DECRS Compliance:** Any partner supplying API for US market must maintain active registration in the FDA Drug Establishments Current Registration Site. Mismatch between COA-issuing entity and registered site is a red flag in partner procurement due diligence.
2. **VICH Alignment:** CMC studies for EU, US, Japan, Australia, Canada, UK markets must follow VICH guidelines; China-specific guidelines may differ.
3. **Stability:** Real-time and accelerated stability studies required for both API and finished product before CMC technical section submission.
4. **Analytical Controls:** Assay, impurities, sterility (injectable), related substances, residual solvents, elemental impurities — all require validated methods prior to release.
5. **GMP:** All clinical and commercial material must be manufactured in GMP-compliant facilities.
6. **Environmental Impact & AOI:** Minor technical sections required as part of CNADA package.
