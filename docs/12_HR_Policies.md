---

## 1. EQUAL OPPORTUNITY & NON-DISCRIMINATION

Byrock is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, colour, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, nationality, disability, marital status, or any other protected status.

## 2. CODE OF CONDUCT

Employees and contractors must act with integrity, honesty, and professionalism. High ethical standards apply in interactions with veterinary clinics, regulators, licence partners, and investors.

## 3. WORKPLACE HARASSMENT

Zero-tolerance policy. Complaints treated confidentially. Compliant with applicable employment and equality legislation.

## 4. DATA PROTECTION & CLINICAL PRIVACY

All personnel handling PTP-102 clinical data and biomarkers must comply with GDPR, clinical trial regulations, and company data governance policies.

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY & IP PROTECTION

All employees, consultants, and licence-partner contacts must maintain confidentiality of PTP-102 technology, biomarker assays, regulatory submissions, and partner terms.

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All inventions, software, documentation, and know-how created during employment are the sole property of Byrock Clinical Ltd. Employees may be required to sign IP Assignment Agreements.

## 7. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Continuous feedback and structured performance reviews. Individuals are expected to contribute to team and organisational goals.

## 8. ATTENDANCE & WORKING HOURS

Punctuality and adherence to agreed working patterns are required. Flexible and hybrid arrangements may be available for clinical / regulatory roles where compatible with trial workflows.

## 9. COMPENSATION & BENEFITS

Compensation is paid per terms of employment. Benefits review annually. Equity / incentive participation may apply per the ESOP / Incentive Agreement.

## 10. SEPARATION & EXIT

Resignation or termination must follow employment terms. Employees must complete exit formalities and return all Company property, data, and credentials.

---

## APPLICABILITY

These policies apply to all employees, consultants, contractors, interns, and secondees associated with Byrock Clinical Ltd and its licence partners.
