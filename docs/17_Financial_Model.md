## KEY ASSUMPTIONS

### Market & Demand
- **Target Market Population:** 12,714,947 (global equine population in addressable markets)
- **Laminitis Case Rate:** 10%
- **Global TAM (cases):** ~1,197,000 declining ~1.5% annually (equine population trends)
- **SAM %:** 50% → 80% over 4 years (growing market share / penetration)
- **SAM (cases):** ~598,600 in Year 1, rising to ~1,094,000 by Year 5

### Pricing & Unit Economics
- **Price per Treatment:** $1,000 USD
- **Cost per Treatment:** $35.12 USD
- **Gross Margin per Treatment:** ~96.5%
- **Average Doses Required:** 2 per treatment course
- **Average ML per Dose:** ~486 mL

### Manufacturing Cost Structure
- **100 Kg compound cost:** $722,696
- **Final product volume:** 20,000,000 mL
- **Cost per mL:** $0.0361
- **Cost per Dose:** $17.56

### Operating Margins (Industry Baseline)
- **COGS:** ~36% of revenue (derived from industry-average pharma margins, Damodaran / Capital IQ)
- **Gross Margin:** 63%
- **Operating Expense:** ~27-29% of revenue (S&M, R&D, G&A blended)
- **Operating Margin:** ~18-20% (late-stage discovery benchmarking)

### Investment Phases (Laminitis Financials)

| Startup | Pre-production | $135,000 | Facility, equipment, QC, regulatory docs |
| Phase 1 | 1–6 months | $3,591,800 | Production, testing, packaging, marketing |

### Deal Structure Guidance (Wellspring EMEA / Licensing Executives Society)
- **Rule of thumb:** Licensor receives ~25% of value captured by licensee.
- **Implied royalty on sales:** 4.5–5% (based on 18–20% operating margin).
- **Stage:** Late discovery → expect lower upfront, upside-aligned milestones.
- **Comparable partners / market signals:** Key questions are TAM validity, manufacturing margin outlook, and deal structure expectations.
