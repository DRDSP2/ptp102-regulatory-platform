We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information accessed through the PTP-102 Licence Partner Portal.

---

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

- **Account data:** Name, email, company, regulatory licence number.
- **Usage data:** Portal interactions, document access logs, session metadata.
- **Clinical / business data:** Only data voluntarily uploaded or created by authorised partners (e.g. trial site metrics, invoice details).
- **Technical data:** Device, IP, browser, and diagnostic logs for security and service improvement.

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- Operating and improving the Service and PTP-102 platform.
- Processing transactions, renewals, and licence administration.
- Personalising partner-facing dashboards and communications.
- Compliance with regulatory obligations.

## 3. SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share with:
- Trusted service providers (hosting, payment, analytics) bound by similar confidentiality.
- Regulatory or veterinary authorities where required by law.
- Other Byrock group entities or advisers under equivalent safeguards.

## 4. DATA SECURITY

Technical and organisational measures are implemented to protect data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## 5. DATA RETENTION

Data is retained only as long as necessary for regulatory, commercial, and legal purposes, unless a longer period is required by law.

## 6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict processing where applicable.

## 7. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use session and functional cookies for authentication, security, and service analytics. Preferences can be managed in your browser settings.

## 8. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 9. TRANSFER OF DATA

Data may be transferred to locations outside your jurisdiction. Appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, regulatory approvals) are in place.

## 10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Policy. Changes are published on the portal and effective on posting. Material changes will be notified to licence contacts by email.
